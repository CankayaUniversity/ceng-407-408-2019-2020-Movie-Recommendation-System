Movie Recommendation System

About Our Project

Our aim is to develop a software called "Movie Recommendation System", which is a web-based application that uses various prediction algorithms(machine learning) to propose their users' new movies according to their movie tastes. In addition to that, the system also proposes new movies to its users based on their friends' movie preferences. Also, the web-based application will be compatible with mobile devices too. The purpose of this project is to help people to find new movies to watch and share their movie interests with other people and with their friends.
